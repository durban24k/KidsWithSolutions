About Us

KWSF is a social enterprise that aims to cultivate the next generation of Innovators, Inventors, and Change-Makers through its unique programs that equip young people with the core discovery skills that drive innovation.

Our Team

Our Services

KWSF has pursued several ambitious goals from developing curriculums to rapidly accelerate the roll out of digital literacy skills to strengthen the Information Technology workforce in low income countries and financial literacy skills to empower young people to make sound financial decisions

Leadership Skills

To empower our youthful generation with the skills and tools to thrive in the 21st century

IT Skills

To increase the level of digital literacy skills amongst children in key low income countries and pursue significant structural gaps in IT

Competence

To leverage on Information Technology to provide a level playing for the young people in the underprivileged societies in Africa

Community Service

To increase confidence amongst children and young people to be the solution providers in their own societies
